About Jack McKenna

Raised in Amersham, I am very familiar with the South Buckinghamshire area. After early beggings in the world of sound engineering, I decided to change my path. Though I still have a passion for Music, I now know my true passion is electricity!

When working as a sound engineer, I was taking part time jobs here and there

I am now a fully insured NICEIC certified electrician. I can offer all types of electrical work from troubleshooting and repair, to design and installation. Whether you need a socket outlet relocated, a consumer unit updated to regulation, or a complete rewire, there is no job too big or small.
As a registered Domestic Installer with the NICEIC, all work is covered by the NICEIC Platinum Promise and issued with relevant certification.
As well as specialising in electrical work, I am competent in decorating and basic plastering, which means I can “make good” any chases or holes that may be required to complete the electrical install.
